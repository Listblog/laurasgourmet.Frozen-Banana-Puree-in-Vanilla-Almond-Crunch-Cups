Frozen Banana Puree in Vanilla Almond Crunch Cups Recipe
By Chef Laura Briscoe
Prep time: 10 minutes Cook time: 2 Hours Servings: 5 cups

Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Vanilla Almond Crunch

- 1/2 ounce plant based butter

- 1 large banana, peeled

Instructions

  1. Place granola in a food processor and puree coarsely ground.
  2. Add butter and pulse until combined.
  3. Using a Â½ ounce cookie scoop or a one tablespoon measure, divide the granola base into muffin liners in a mini muffin tin.
  4. Press firmly to form a base.
  5. Puree banana until smooth.
  6. Portion 2 Tablespoons, 23 grams or .80 ounces over the base in each cup.
  7. Freeze for 2 hours over overnight.
  8. When ready to enjoy, remove from freezer and remove wrapper.
  9. Drizzle with melted chocolate if feeling decadent
